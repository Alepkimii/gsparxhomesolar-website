Our Story

Powering Malaysia's Shift To Solar, One Roof At A Time

A wholly-owned subsidiary of Tenaga Nasional Berhad, engineering accessible solar for every electricity user in the country.

Since 2018

Built On TNB's 70-Year Legacy

GSPARX Sdn. Bhd. was established in 2018 as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Tenaga Nasional Berhad — Malaysia's national utility, with over 70 years powering the country. We were built with one mandate: make rooftop solar accessible, engineered properly, and genuinely affordable for every electricity user in Malaysia, from a single terrace house to a full industrial facility.

Today we design, supply, install and maintain complete solar PV ecosystems — on-grid, hybrid and off-grid — supported by Solar ATAP registration, Net Energy Metering (NEM) guidance, and nationwide after-sales care. Our online proposal tool now brings that same engineering rigor to your browser: real sizing, real pricing, real options.

Accelerate Malaysia's transition to clean, self-generated energy by making solar simple, bankable and built to last.
